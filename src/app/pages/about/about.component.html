About

- How typing speed is calculated?

Typing speed is calculated by dividing the total number of correctly typed characters by 4, which represents the average word length in English. This approach helps to minimize the impact of random factors caused by varying word lengths. By focusing on characters rather than words, we obtain a more precise measurement of typing speed.

- Why this website was created?

This website was created with the purpose of offering a professional tool to help individuals learn fast and accurate keyboard typing. Its development aims to provide a platform where users can enhance their typing skills in an efficient and effective manner.

- Who can benefit from this platform?

Whether you are a student, professional, or simply someone looking to improve their typing skills, this platform is designed to meet a wide range of needs. The typing exercises and performance tracking tools benefit both beginners, who can start at their own pace, and advanced typists, who want to refine their skills and aim for higher accuracy and speed. Our mission is to make typing practice engaging, accessible, and rewarding for everyone.
